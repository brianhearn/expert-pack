# Decisions Index

Three key decisions to make before buying hardware. Getting these right upfront saves significant time and money.

| Decision | File | Make This Decision Before |
|----------|------|--------------------------|
| **Installation Method** | [installation-method.md](installation-method.md) | Buying hardware |
| **Hardware Selection** | [hardware-selection.md](hardware-selection.md) | Ordering anything |
| **Protocol Selection** | [protocol-selection.md](protocol-selection.md) | Buying smart devices or a coordinator |

**Short answers for the impatient:**
- **Installation method:** HA OS, unless you have a NAS (Container) or specific Linux needs (Supervised)
- **Hardware:** Raspberry Pi 5 + USB SSD, or a used Intel N100 mini-PC
- **Protocol:** Zigbee for most devices + ESPHome for custom sensors
